## Terms of Use for Municipal Implementation

### For Copenhagen Municipality:
This software is provided to Copenhagen Municipality with full rights to use, modify, and deploy for municipal purposes, including public consultation and urban planning activities.

### For Other Municipalities:
Other municipalities are encouraged to adapt this project for their own use under the MIT License terms. When doing so:

1. Update branding and municipal information appropriately
2. Ensure compliance with local data protection regulations
3. Maintain attribution to the original project
4. Consider contributing improvements back to the community

### Data Collection and Privacy:
- All analytics data is stored locally in the user's browser
- No personal information is collected without explicit consent
- Users can clear their data at any time
- Municipal implementations should provide clear privacy policies

### Commercial Use:
While the code is open source under MIT License, municipalities implementing this for public consultation should ensure:
- Transparent use of technology in public decision-making
- Accessible alternatives for citizens without AR-capable devices
- Clear communication about how feedback will be used in municipal decisions
